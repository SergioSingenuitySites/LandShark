Answers before they hesitate.

These expandable FAQs open right where guests click, so nothing feels broken or buried below the fold.

How long is the tour?

The signature Land Shark experience is a personally narrated 90-minute land-and-sea tour.

Where do tours depart?

Tours depart from 99 West Cabrillo Boulevard in Santa Barbara, near the beachside corner of Cabrillo Boulevard and Chapala Street.

Is it good for kids?

Yes. The ride is designed as an all-ages sightseeing experience with city views, harbor time, and a fun splash moment.

Can we book a private event?

Yes. Private charters are a strong fit for birthdays, schools, reunions, corporate outings, and group celebrations.

Should we book ahead?

Yes. Tours can sell out, especially during busy travel periods. This mockup makes availability checking the primary action.

What should we bring?

Comfortable clothes, sun protection, and a camera. The experience is designed to be easy, scenic, and memorable.
